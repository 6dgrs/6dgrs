Safety Centre

Meet With Trust

6dgrs is built around mutual paths, controlled visibility, and verified real-world interactions.

How 6dgrs keeps trust

People are discovered through Trusted Friends and mutual paths, not open public feeds.

Meetup safety tips

Meet in public places, keep plans small, share context with trusted friends, and only unlock networks after a real meetup.

Verification methods

Use QR scan, same-location checks, or timed mutual confirmation to prove a meetup happened in person.

Reporting and blocking

Reports stay private. Blocking removes the person from your visible trusted graph.

After a verified meetup

Meetups create Met first. Wider network unlock only happens when both people accept a Trusted Friend relationship and a slot is available.
